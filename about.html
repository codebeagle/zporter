About

Thanks for taking a few minutes to learn more about me. I am a designer, developer, adventurer, husband, and dad living in Utah. Beyond design I love overlanding and camping, food, gaming, and a whole lot more.

Core Values

I have spent much of my life learning first hand the values of hard work and integrity. I grew up with the Scouting program and learned invaluable lessons in leadership and self-development which has helped me become a top acheiver in many areas of my life. I strive for personal growth, authenticity, and helping those in need.

I believe in doing the right thing and leaving things better than I found them. I believe design can be used to help make life better for everyone and solve some of the biggest issues facing humanity today.
