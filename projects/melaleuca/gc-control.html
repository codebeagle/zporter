GC Control Microsite

GC Control Microsite

Role

Design

Tools

Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator

Summary

Product launch microsite for annual convention

The Challenge

My first microsite design as a web designer came when I was tasked with helping Melaleuca launch a new product called "GC Control." This product had a large amount of testing and research done with a variety of health benefits and success stories. This site needed to share all of that plus essential information for Melaleuca's customers and would be featured at the annual customer convention.

The Solution

I designed a single-page microsite with a tab navigation to allow the users quick access to the various content categories. As a user moved through the tabs there was a smooth parallax trasition where the elements would move at different speeds on and off of the viewport. After design work was complete, I handed it off to a senior designer to build. This site was very successful and helped to create demand for the product beyond the initail projections.
