International Sites

Roles

Design

Front-end Development

Tools

Adobe Photoshop, HTML, CSS, Javascript

Summary

Created banner ads, landing pages, and launched sites in new countries globally.

The Challenge

Melaleluca had grown a successful online store in the US and needed to expand it to customers worldwide. I was hired to work on the international sites which needed to follow the US site and be localized while also serving unique content to those in country.

The Solution

I began by working on the first existing international site, which was for customers in Australia and New Zealand. I designed banner ads, landing pages, monthly promotion pages, and enhancements to the shopping experience. Eventually I assisted in launching similar sites for european customers in english, dutch, german, and french. Once those were completed, I then mentored additional designers while launching sites for Singapore, Malaysia, Japan, and China.
