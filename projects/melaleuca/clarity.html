Clarity Microsite

Role

Design

Front-end Development

Tools

Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Flash

Summary

Designed a product launch microsite for the annual convention.

The Challenge

Melaleuca had developed a new line of products that removes acne and heals skin. I was asked to create a microsite experience similar to GC Control to launch the new product at the annual customer convention. It needed to be interactive and minimal in style while still sharing the information needed.

The Solution

Using GC Control as a starting point, I designed an experience using the concept of blur and focus to tell a story about clear skin and self confidence. Due to the limitation of web technologies, I used Adobe Flash to create the site with the desired effects and animations.
