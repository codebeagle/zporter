Learn to Earn

Role

Design

Tools

Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Marvel

Summary

Digital customer education application with reward system.

The Challenge

Melaleuca's market research team launched several campaigns and tools to help educate newly onboarded customers and sales reps, such as custom product stories, and one of those was to build an online education application. This tool needed to provide rewards to users for watching videos about Melaleuca products and taking small quizzes.

The Solution

I worked through many iterations of requirement gathering, wire frames, interactive prototypes, and stakeholder presentations to produce the designs shown here. The final designs were handed off to the development team to build while I assisted in communicating the functionality and requirements. The final result looked great and is still relied on heavily to onboard new customers and sales reps.
