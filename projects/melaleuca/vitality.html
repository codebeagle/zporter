Vitality Microsite

Vitality Microsite

Roles

Design

Front-end Development

Tools

Adobe Photoshop, HTML, CSS, Javascript

Summary

Designed and built a microsite for a health-centric marketing initiative.

The Challenge

Melaleuca's health-focused product brand, Vitality for Life, started a large-scale marketing initative to produce a monthly magazine and asked my team to build a website to support it. The site needed to provide valuable resources to customers and help create interest in the health products Melaleuca offers. Additionally, it needed to help promote the identity of the Vitality campaign.

The Solution

I created this site using the Vitality brand elements and organized the extensive amount of content into an interface that could be easy to navigate while remaining scalable for future content. As the content and resources continued to be produced, I expanded the site to include new types of content and interactive features.
