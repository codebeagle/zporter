100 Product Stories

100 Product Stories

Roles

Design

Front-end development

Tools

Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, HTML, CSS, and Javascript

Summary

Multiple product story designs and build projects for a year-long team goal.

The Challenge

Melaleuca's marketing research team found that there were many new sales reps being onboarded to the company but they didn't have much knowledge of the products they were selling and there wasn't an easy way to self educate. Along with the Learn to Earn campaign, my team was asked to design custom product stories that lived on the product pages. These stories needed to give history, features, and scientific data about the products so users could learn about the products on their own while they shopped. We were given a challenge to complete 100 of these story projects in a single year.

The Solution

I worked alongside other designers, front-end developers, and our creative director to deliver 100 of these pages within the year. For each story I researched the product, worked with the product manager to assemble content, design the experience, and write the production code. These stories continue to educate and inspire sales reps to this day.
