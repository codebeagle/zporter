Brave Pride

Brave Pride

Role

Design

Tool

Adobe Photoshop

Summary

Designed a yearbook cover for Illahae High School.

The Challenge

One of the yearbook staff members at Illahae High School had envisioned a cover design using broadway signage and associating different activities to popular shows. The design request here was to use that idea to produce a vibrant and colorful cover.

The Solution

This cover required extensive photo manipulation and color grading. I used the design ideas from the yearbook staff, as well as a few ideas of my own, to create an unforgettable recreation of broadway.
