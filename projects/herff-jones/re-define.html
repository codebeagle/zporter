Redefine

Redefine

Role

Design

Tools

Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator

Summary

Yearbook cover and endsheet designs for Saint Francis School.

The Challenge

The students at Saint Francis School experienced significant school remodelling in 2009 and decided to use that as inspiration for their yearbook. The theme "redefine" aligned with the chance the students had to start fresh at in a new environment next year.

The Solution

My design approach here was to continue to play on the idea of controlled chaos by combining the statue of Saint Francis as the focal point in a mix of chaotic symbols and elements. This colorful approach created an interested and exciting mood.
