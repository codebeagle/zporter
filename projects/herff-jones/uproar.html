Uproar

Uproar

Role

Design

Tools

Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign

Summary

Designed yearbook assets for Mark Morris High School.

The Challenge

The yearbook staff at Mark Morris High School wanted their yearbook to make a bold visual statement using their theme, "Uproar." The design needed to set a tone of celebration and energy with music as a visual element. The design package was order included a cover, front endsheet, title page, and division page design.

The Solution

To acheive the target look and feel, I chose to use vibrant colors and lines eminating from the musician. To enhance the contrast and impact I relied heavily on stark black and white shapes and white space. Though challenging to output due to gamut limitation, the final book was produced to have rich color throughout.
