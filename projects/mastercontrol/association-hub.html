Summary

Designed a microsite to showcase MasterControl association partnerships

The Challenge

MasterControl's association partnerships grew significantly in 2019 and to support them we needed a location within our site to showcase partnerships and events for recruiting. The project also needed to provide shareable details for specific association events.

The Solution

I designed and built a custom microsite within mastercontrol.com with easy-to-update and maintain pages. These pages gave an easily accessible avenue for future partners to view events and sign up to attend. Eventually, this section of our marketing merged with other teams as the COVID-19 pandemic began.
