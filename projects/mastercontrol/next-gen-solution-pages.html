Next Gen Solution Pages

Roles

Design

Front-end Development

Tools

Adobe XD, HTML, CSS, Javascript

Summary

A series of landing page design for a new sales model.

The Challenge

MasterControl needed to introduce a new sales model with multiple cloud-based solutions and custom pricing and packages for each. I was asked to create a landing page template for these solution pages in a clean and branded format.

The Solution

I developed a template that gave a high-level overview of each solution that enabled a prospective customer to browse through the products and self-educate at a high level. This approach worked especially well with the simultaneous home page update that led them here.
