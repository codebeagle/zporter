Summary

Created custom page components to create a unified experience.

The Challenge

Our digital marketing team needed a way to unify our industry landing pages. As part of the task, we also needed to find a better way to explore multiple industries.

The Solution

The challenge was met easily through the creation of new page components. I designed a new hero blade and new navigation blade that when added to pages allowed for lateral navigation between industries. Several dynamic animations were used to enhance the flow and tie the industry pages to the navigation through color.
