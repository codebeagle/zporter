Pricing Page Update

Roles

Design

Front-end Development

Tools

Figma, HTML, CSS, Javascript

Summary

Web page design for pricing information requests.

The Challenge

One of the most common questions a potential Saas customer has is how much will the software cost and MasterControl needed to update their pricing page. It needed to provide a clear path to requesting pricing information and reinforce the product packages available.

The Solution

This page addresses the challenge through an interactive region for browsing product packages and a prominent pricing request form. The content on the page answers the basic questions and the overall flow allows a user to freely scan.
