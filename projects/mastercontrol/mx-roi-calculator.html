The Challenge

Before the MasterControl rebrand I worked on a project to create an interactive ROI calculator. With the rebrand complete, I was tasked to return to the project and complete the design using the new brand guidelines with high interactivity.

The Solution

Many of the structural decisions were made in the previous project, but the project was lacking something. I used new brand elements to design the calculator with an animating cube and strong path to capturing leads for our sales team.
