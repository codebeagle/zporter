Med Device Trends Brief

Roles

Design

Front-end Development

Tools

Figma, HTML, CSS, Javascript

Summary

Designed a single page microsite to promote a new digital asset.

The Challenge

The MasterControl creative team created a new asset for the Med Device industry and I was tasked with taking that asset and creating a digital experience out of it. This microsite needed to share the content of the asset and engage the users.

The Solution

I decided to deviate from our traditional approach and leverage the screen real estate available. I used the full viewport combined with animated transitions to create content slides as a single-page experience. Multiple navigation methods make it easy to move through the slides and provide a final slide to download the original asset.
