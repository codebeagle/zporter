Summary

A collection of support materials for sales reps and retailers.

The Challenge

Aquamira's sales team needed resources to recruit, onboard, and educate retailers. My team was tasked on a regular basis with designing materials for our reps to use to acheive their goals.

The Solution

I worked closely with our COO, CEO, and sales team members to design assets that met their needs. These publications were regularly revised so version control and flexibility became part of every design.
