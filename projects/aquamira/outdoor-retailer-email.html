Outdoor Retailer Email

Outdoor Retailer Email

Roles

Design

Tools

Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Mailchimp

Summary

Designed a custom email campaign for the annual Outdoor Retailer trade show.

The Challenge

Aquamira attended the 2017 Outdoor Retailer show and wanted to help drive retailers to the booth at the show and communicate new product announcements when the show ended. This email campaign alse needed to promote the updated brand family and product line.

The Solution

I designed, built, and launched this email campaign to Aquamira retailers as two separate blasts. The first occured a few days before the trade show and included an invitation to the booth. The second came after the show ended and included product announcements, special offers, and a primary call-to-action to download the new product line information and order forms.
