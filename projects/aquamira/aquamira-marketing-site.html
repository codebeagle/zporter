Summary

Marketing website overhaul to improve online presence.

The Challenge

My first assignment at Aquamira was to take the lead on a full website overhaul, including information architecture, content creation, and web development. The site needed the ability to educate customers about the products and create a unique brand presence online.

The Solution

I worked closely with our marketing director, the COO, and the CEO to create an entirely new site structure with navigation options for content, products, and company information. I created wireframes and high-fidelity designs for approval before fully writing all the code for the site. I then integrated analytics and Wordpress as a content management system before launching it to the public.
