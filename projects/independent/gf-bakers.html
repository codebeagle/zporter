G.F. Bakers

Roles

Design

Front-end Development

Tools

Adobe XD, HTML, CSS, Javascript, PHP, Wordpress

Summary

E-commerce website for an independent gluten-free baker.

The Challenge

G.F. Bakers was just starting their business selling gluten-free baked good and mixes directly to consumers and soon to retailers. The website needed to showcase their products and provide the ability for people to place orders online.

The Solution

I worked alongside my client and another contracted graphic designer to design and build their website. The site used the WooCommerce platform to handle e-commerce functionality while the content did the storytelling. This build was successful and eventually migrated to an SEO agency to help promote and grow after launching.

This site was designed using Figma and crafted by hand using HTML, CSS, and Javascript.
