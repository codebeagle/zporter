First Aid Inserts

Role

Design

Tools

Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign

Summary

Designed and produced a custom product insert for first aid products being sold on amazon.com.

The Challenge

The client was in search of some help desiging and producing a simple insert to go inside a custom product he developed. The first aid kits would be sold on amazon.com and this insert would need to provide the customer with the necessary information of what is included in the kit and how to use it.

The Solution

I gathered the content and requirements from the customer and determined the final dimensions of the artwork. I worked with the client to finalize a design that could be adapted in the future should the product line up change. These kits were eventually sold and used his unique blend of products and instructions to provide fast and immediate response to injury.

This site was designed using Figma and crafted by hand using HTML, CSS, and Javascript.
