Custom Tattoo

Role

Design

Tools

Pen and Ink, Watercolor, Adobe Photoshop

Summary

Custom tatto design for a personal friend.

The Challenge

I was approached by a personal friend to design a tattoo that would carry special symbolism and important significance. The artwork needed to be ready to hand off to a tattoo artist and tell the story he wanted to in the style he desired.

The Solution

I worked on multiple iterations of the design, starting with thumbnail sketches and leading up to production art. To acheive the final result, I combined digital painting with traditional pen drawing and watercolor textures. The result was a beautiful and powerful visual statement.

This site was designed using Figma and crafted by hand using HTML, CSS, and Javascript.
